WELCOME TO THE Great Value Academy Fraternity

Thank you for your interest in Great Value Academy, a dynamic Christian international school in beautiful Nairobi, Kenya. Our school has a rich 75-year history of serving families in East Africa. Great Value Academy currently serves approximately 650 students from over 50 countries. This diversity is exciting and enriching for our community, but our commonly held commitments and sense of partnership is what truly unites us into a strong community.

As a community, we commit to our Mission:

Great Value Academy inspires and equips each student to develop their God-given gifts for Christ-like service in the world community.

Our passion is to see our students inspired and equipped for service during their time at Great Value Academy and throughout their lives. Our students learn about the love of Christ and the Christian worldview, exploring what it means to learn with excellence, form deep relationships, and serve others for the glory of God. It is so exciting for us to see our graduates go on to success in university and in their communities around the world, building upon the solid foundation of their Great Value Academy education. We are deeply committed to this mission and purpose.

As you explore our website, I hope you will do so with a growing sense of excitement about Great Value Academy. Please contact us if you are a parent looking for a school for your child, a teacher looking for an opportunity to serve God in a dynamic Christian community, or an alumnus looking to reconnect.

For His Glory,

Dr. Ian Makumi
Superintendent
