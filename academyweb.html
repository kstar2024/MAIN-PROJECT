Our Education Model

We understand the inevitable fact that, as each day passes by, the world is being taken over by technology. For this reason, we incorporate technology into our students’ education. We have adopted various learning management systems such as Coolsis, Google Suite for Education, and Zoom.

These software solutions complement physical classes by offering platforms for teachers to assign homework and tests to students, as well as enabling teachers to create fun quizzes that help stimulate students in class.

Parents can track their students’ educational progress and discipline, as academic grades and discipline reports are available in these systems.

Adaptation During the COVID-19 Pandemic

During the COVID-19 pandemic, many students worldwide were unable to attend school due to mandatory lockdowns. However, our students were not disadvantaged, as we easily transitioned to a fully online education model. All our staff are technology-savvy, and our goal is to ensure that every student is very computer literate by the time they leave our school. This skill is essential in today’s world.
